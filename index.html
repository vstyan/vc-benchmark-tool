Smoothness Rating

Evaluates visual consistency based on the ratio of Jank Frames to Total Frames.

  • Grade A: < 1% Jank
  • Grade B: < 5% Jank
  • Grade C: < 15% Jank
  • Grade D: > 15% Jank
Total Jank

A Jank Frame is defined as any frame that takes longer than 33.3ms to render.

In a 30FPS video conference, these delayed frames cause visible stuttering and audio/video desync.
